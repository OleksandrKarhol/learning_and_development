Our Story

Since 2012, Timber & Thread has been fusing craftsmanship with material intelligence. Each collection reflects an interest in structure, comfort, and sustainable sourcing — from FSC-certified timber to low-emission finishes.

  • 01
    Material intelligence
    We choose materials for durability and tactile comfort, testing for longevity before production.
  • 02
    Human-centered design
    Our pieces are ergonomically considered, ensuring long-term comfort without sacrificing aesthetics.
