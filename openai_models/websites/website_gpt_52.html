About Grain & Form

We’re a furniture company focused on durable hardwood pieces with clean geometry and honest materials. Our process blends cabinetmaker discipline with modern design workflows—prototypes, stress points, finish tests, and repeatable jigs to keep quality consistent.

Sourcing

FSC-aligned suppliers when available, preference for local mills, and transparency on species + origin.

Build philosophy

Fewer parts, smarter joints, serviceable hardware—so your piece is repairable instead of disposable.

“Blueprint to living room”
