About Arc & Grain

We’re a small furniture studio blending traditional woodworking with parametric design. Our “non-standard” design language: soft arcs, exposed joinery, and modular silhouettes that adapt to modern living.

Material-first design
Every curve follows grain direction to reduce waste and boost strength.
Micro-batch production
Limited runs with rapid iteration—like software, but in wood.
Repairable by design
Parts are replaceable; finishes are easy to refresh.

Design System: “Arc Logic”

We use a curve-and-grid system that creates visual calm while keeping load paths strong. It’s furniture that looks experimental—but behaves predictably.
