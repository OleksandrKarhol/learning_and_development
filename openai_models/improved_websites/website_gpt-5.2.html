About

A studio practice, not a factory.

Atelier Grove was founded to make furniture that holds attention quietly: clean lines, readable structure, and surfaces that wear gracefully. We work in small runs and take on select made-to-order pieces.

Our hardwoods are chosen for stability and story—FSC-certified where possible, supplemented by reclaimed stock from regional demolition yards. Grain orientation and seasonal movement are considered from the first sketch.

Process

Three steps, repeated until everything feels inevitable.

  1. Step 01

    Design

    Proportion studies, grain planning, and a simple spec sheet you can keep.

  2. Step 02

    Build

    Careful milling, tight joinery, and dry fitting to align faces and reveals.

  3. Step 03

    Finish

    Hand-sanded surfaces, low-VOC finishes, and final inspection under raking light.

Sustainability

Sourced with restraint.

We prioritize certified lumber, reclaimed stock, and local mills. Offcuts are repurposed for small goods, packaging is paper-first, and finishes are selected for indoor air quality.

Traceability: species, origin, and mill documented.

Longevity: repairable finishes, replaceable hardware.
