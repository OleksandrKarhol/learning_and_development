Story

Atelier Grove began with a simple premise: form follows function, and function should endure. We source hardwoods from responsibly managed forests and small mills, where the emphasis is on traceable origin and minimal processing. Our pieces are shaped by hand and finished with wax and oil to reveal the wood’s natural character.

For generations, furniture was built to last. We honor that tradition by slowing the pace of production, refining each detail, and letting the grain breathe. The result is a collection of quiet, precise forms that age with grace.

Process

Design

We begin with measurements and intent, sketching around how the piece will be used.

Build

Timber is milled, joints are crafted by hand, and a quiet frame takes shape.

Finish

Finishes are hand-applied to enhance grain and resilience, then cured in a controlled environment.

We believe sustainability and craft belong together. Our studio partners with mills that share a respect for forests and the people who tend them.
