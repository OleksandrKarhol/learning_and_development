Notes from the bench

A small atelier with a long view.

Atelier Grove was founded to make furniture that holds up to attention—proportion you notice, details you feel, and materials that age with grace.

We work with responsibly managed mills and small suppliers, choosing boards for stability and grain. Every piece begins with a clear set of constraints: honest structure, minimal hardware, and finishes that can be renewed.

Our approach is editorial—less ornament, more intent. The result is furniture that belongs in calm spaces and busy lives alike.

Built in-house, start to finish.

We keep production small to protect the details: consistent reveals, clean interior corners, and edges that feel intentional to the hand.

Micro-detail

Before finishing, every edge gets a subtle “break”—a controlled softening that prevents chipping and improves touch without dulling the line.

Sustainability, without theatrics

We prioritize domestic hardwoods from responsibly managed forests, design for repair, and choose finishes that can be renewed rather than stripped. Offcuts become jigs, small objects, or packaging protection—waste is treated as a design constraint.
