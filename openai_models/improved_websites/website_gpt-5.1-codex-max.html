Our Story

Rooted in the slow craft of wood

Atelier Grove began in a one-room workshop where prototypes were drawn on kraft paper and refined by hand. Today, we remain small by design, building limited runs to honor the wood and the people who live with it.

We partner with regional mills that steward forests responsibly. Each board is air-dried, acclimated to our studio, and matched so grain and tone flow across every surface.

Process in three movements

01

Design

Scaled drawings, mockups, and joinery studies to balance form and function.

02

Build

Hand-joined frames, sequenced panels, and meticulous surface prep.

03

Finish

Layered oil-wax finishes cured slowly for a breathable, repairable sheen.

S

Sustainability, in practice

Offcuts are re-joined into panels for small goods, sawdust is donated to local farms, and finishes are VOC-free. We repair what we build to extend each piece’s life.
