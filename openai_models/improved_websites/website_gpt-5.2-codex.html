Our Craft

Atelier Grove began as a two-person workshop focused on making furniture that feels contemporary yet enduring. Every design starts with a proportion study and ends with a tactile finish applied by hand.

We source hardwoods from local mills and small forest cooperatives. Each board is selected for stability and grain character, then rested before milling to ensure quiet longevity.

Process

01 — Design

Sketches, scale models, and proportions tuned to the room.

02 — Build

Hand-cut joinery and careful grain alignment.

03 — Finish

Natural oils and waxes for depth and repairability.
