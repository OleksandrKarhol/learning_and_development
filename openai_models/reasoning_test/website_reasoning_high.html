ABOUT

Craft, sourcing, and restraint.

Atelier Grove is a small studio making furniture that privileges structure and proportion. We work in solid hardwoods, designed to weather daily life and remain serviceable for years.

Story

We began with a simple question: what would a contemporary heirloom look like if every line was earned? The answer became a practice—sketching, prototyping, and refining until the piece feels calm in the room and clear in the hand.

Our hardwoods come from responsible suppliers and small mills with traceable chains of custody. We choose boards for character and stability, then build with joinery that supports movement rather than fighting it.

Micro-detail: we orient grain direction to reduce visual noise across faces, aligning cathedrals and rays for continuity.

Process

Three steps, repeated with care.

DESIGN • BUILD • FINISH

  1. 01

    Design

    Proportions, clearances, and a measured palette of details—validated with samples.

  2. 02

    Build

    Milled for stability; joinery cut cleanly. Dry-fits ensure alignment before glue-up.

  3. 03

    Finish

    Low-VOC oils and waxes, hand-rubbed in thin coats for depth and repairability.

Sustainability

We prioritize FSC-certified and reclaimed stock when appropriate, optimize cut lists to reduce waste, and select finishes with low VOCs. Offcuts are repurposed into small objects or donated to local craft programs.

SUBTLE COMMITMENT

Durability is the most sustainable feature: pieces designed for maintenance, not disposal.

Ask us for material provenance on any build.
