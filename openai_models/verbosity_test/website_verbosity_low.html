A small atelier, a clear standard.

Atelier Grove is a furniture maker and retailer focused on modern heirlooms—pieces that age gracefully, feel composed, and hold up under real use.

What we optimize for

  • —Grain continuity and calm surfaces
  • —Strong joinery with minimal hardware
  • —Repairability and future refinishing

Story

We began as a two-person workshop building custom casework—learning that the best work is often the quietest: edges that soften light, drawers that close without comment, proportions that simply feel right.

Our hardwoods are chosen for stability and character, sourced from regional mills and certified suppliers when available. We document species, finish schedules, and care guidance so each piece can be maintained for years—not replaced.

Sustainability

We prioritize long-lived construction and repairable finishes. When a piece is built to be refinished—not replaced—its footprint changes.

We source from regional mills when possible, select certified stock as available, and design around yield to reduce waste. Offcuts become jigs, test panels, and small objects; sawdust is collected for proper disposal.
