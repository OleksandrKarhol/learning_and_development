About

A studio practice with retail clarity.

We build a focused selection of pieces and support tailored dimensions, materials, and finish options for residential and trade clients.

Story

Atelier Grove began as a small workshop dedicated to the fundamentals: sound structure, clean geometry, and surfaces that feel good in the hand. Our work is modern, but the priorities are old—precision, patience, and repeatable methods.

We source hardwoods from suppliers who document origin and practice responsible forestry. Each board is assessed for stability and grain. We then design around the wood, not against it—honoring movement, figure, and the quiet variation that makes each piece singular.

Process

A three-step sequence that keeps decisions calm and the work consistent.

  1. 01

    Design

    We confirm scale, ergonomics, and materials. You’ll receive a concise spec: dimensions, wood, finish, and lead time.

  2. 02

    Build

    Milling, joinery, and dry fitting. Grain is oriented for flow across surfaces and stable movement across seasons.

  3. 03

    Finish

    Low-VOC oils and waxes, built in thin coats. Final assembly and edge work are tuned for touch and light.

Sustainability

Responsible by default

We prioritize documented sourcing, efficient cutting plans, and long-service finishes. Offcuts are reused for small goods and shop fixtures; sawdust is collected for local reuse when possible.

  • Low-VOC finishing systems chosen for indoor air quality.
  • Repairable surfaces—designed for refinishing, not replacement.
  • Durable joinery to extend service life and reduce waste.
